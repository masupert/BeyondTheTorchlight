# Traits
*Characteristics* and *Traits* work together during gameplay to further allow players to define who their character is and how they may act during different situations. *Traits* may be used during combat, role-play, or social situations to inform players how their character may act or in situations to modify the dice roll in a positive or negative way.  

An applicable trait to a situation will add or subtract 1d4 to a  *Difficulty Check* or *Saving throw*. Traits can be both positive and negative to the outcome of a roll.

Players should select one set of traits from each Characteristic group to apply to their character.

### Strength
#### Dominant and Imposing (Rock)
**Description:** Your character possesses an overwhelming physical presence, raw power, and an instinct to assert control. You attempt to physically intimidate, exert brute force in a direct confrontation, or establish immediate authority through sheer presence. You are: *Intimidating, Menacing, Controlling, Provocative*.

- **Strength:** Your raw power or intimidating presence can often overwhelm attempts at finesse or precision. A direct, forceful approach might negate an opponent's careful setup, or your sheer intimidation might prevent them from finding or exploiting your vulnerabilities.
- **Weakness:** Direct force can be absorbed or outlasted by pure endurance and steadfastness. Attempts to overpower an "Unyielding" opponent might be unsuccessful as they simply absorb the blow or hold their ground.

#### Unyielding and Resilient (Paper)
**Description:** You excel at resisting physical pain, holding your ground, and shrugging off impacts. When enduring hardship, or resisting physical pain you push through pain and fatigue. You are: *Gritty, Determined, Stoic, Durable, Resilient*.

- **Strength:** Your ability to withstand intimidation and physical pain allows you remain steadfast where others might be cowed or moved.
- **Weakness:** ==Sheer resilience can be bypassed or broken by precise application of force, exploitation of specific weak points, or clever use of leverage.== 

#### Calculated and Precise (Scissor)
**Description:** Your character understands the mechanics of force, how to apply it efficiently, and use leverage to their advantage in physical tasks. ==You are: Nuanced, Alert, Keen, Sharp,== 

- **Strength:** Your precision and understanding of mechanics allow you to bypass or overcome a purely *Unyielding and Resilient* obstacle by targeting vulnerabilities or using environmental leverage.
- **Weakness:** A *Dominate and Imposing* presence will disrupt your attempts at precision. You can be overwhelmed by raw power. 

### Agility
#### Nimble and Reactive (Rock)
**Description:** Your character is quick to react, possesses swift reflexes, and can reposition themselves with precision. You can perform delicate actions requiring fine motor control and speed. You Are: *Cautious, Graceful, Spry, Quick.*

- **Strength**: Your quick, reactive movements can disrupt an opponent's planned, flowing maneuvers. You might interrupt their smooth action or bypass their graceful defense with sheer speed and unpredictable shifts.
- **Weakness:** Your reactive nature might be outmaneuvered by an opponent who operates entirely outside your immediate perception. Attempts to react to something you don't perceive could lead to an Impairment, as you flail against an unseen threat.

#### Stealthy and Subtle (Paper)
**Description:** You excel at moving unseen, unheard, and unnoticed, blending into the environment or making actions that are difficult to detect. You excel at performing actions that require misdirection or quiet execution. You are: *Elusive, Deceptive, Cautious*. 

- **Strength**: Your ability to remain hidden or perform actions without being noticed bypasses an opponent's quick reflexes, as they cannot react to what they cannot perceive. Your subtle approach prevents them from having an opportunity to react.
- **Weakness:** Your stealthy maneuvers can be difficult to sustain when needing to transition into complex, flowing, or delicate actions.


#### Delicate and Elegant (Scissor)
**Description:** Your character moves with natural grace and efficiency, executing complex and delicate tasks effortlessly.  You can perform sequences of actions that flow seamlessly ranging from dancing, to acrobatics to lock-picking. ==Your are: *Elegant, Graceful, Nimble,*]==

### Fortitude
#### Resolute and Determined (Rock)
**Description:** Your character possesses unwavering willpower, and the ability to maintain focus and resolve in the face of adversity. You have the ability to maintain concentration under physical duress and push through brief periods of exhaustion and pain. You are: *Determined, Inspiring, Stoic, Steadfast.* 

- **Strength:** Your mental resolve can overcome brute physical endurance. You might find the will to continue when pure physicality fails due to pain or despair.
- **Weakness:** With a fixed mindset you struggle to adapt to a changing situation. You may fail to realize you are in pain or being hurt. 


#### Tough and Hardy (Paper)
**Description:** You possess great endurance, and can withstand significant physical punishment or environmental hardships over extended periods. You are: *Gritty, Robust, Durable, Resilient*.

- **Strength:** Your physical hardiness can simply absorb or outlast many physical or environmental hardships. You can push through the long term effects of something that would break a lesser character's will.
- **Weakness:** You often fail to realize the level of pain and physical punishment you are experiencing and you will often choose to continue to endure the pain rather than take measures to remove yourself from it. 


#### ==Adaptive amd Malleable (Scissor)== 
==**Description:** Your character can rapidly adjust to new situations, fluidly shifting their state of mind or physical approach to overcome challenges. Adaptable,== 

- ==**Strength:** Your adaptability allows you to find novel ways to bypass a purely tough opponent's defenses or endurance, by attacking them where they least expect it or by changing the terms of engagement. You don't meet their strength, you go around it.==
- ==**Weakness:** Your adaptability allows you to find novel ways to bypass a purely tough opponent's defenses or endurance, by attacking them where they least expect it or by changing the terms of engagement. You don't meet their strength, you go around it.==


### ==Cunning==
#### ==Insightful and Wise (Rock)==
==**Description:** Your character possesses a keen intellect, sharp observation skills, and an ability to quickly grasp hidden truths, motivations, or patterns.==

- ==**Strength:** Your sharp insight allows you to see through tricks and deceptions, making their attempts at manipulation or misdirection less effective. You perceive the intent behind the illusion.==
- ==**Weakness:** Your intuition can be outmaneuvered by a rigorously planned, methodical approach that leaves no room for intuitive leaps. If you rely on insight against a purely logical and prepared mind, your guesses might be misleading, leading to an Impairment as their structure defeats your intuition.==

#### ==Deceptive and Manipulative (Paper)==
==**Description:** Your character excels at misdirection, charming others, lying, and influencing individuals or groups to their own ends.==

- ==**Strength:** Your ability to mislead can cloud the judgment of even a perceptive individual, making it difficult for them to distinguish truth from fiction. You control the information flow, confusing their insight.==
- ==**Weakness:** Your tricks and charms can be systematically dissected by a logical mind that prioritizes facts and patterns over appearances. An opponent who meticulously analyzes your actions might uncover your deception, leading to an Impairment as your manipulation falls apart under scrutiny.==

#### ==Analytical and Strategic (Scissor)==
==**Description:** Your character possesses a methodical mind, capable of breaking down complex problems, planning ahead, and understanding logical systems==

- ==**Strength:** our logical thinking and planning allow you to see through and dismantle deceptions, as you look for inconsistencies and gaps in the narrative or action. Your strategy anticipates their tricks.==
- ==**Weakness:** Your rigid adherence to logic can be outmaneuvered by an opponent who relies on intuition or operates outside predictable patterns. If you over-analyze an instinctively acting or highly perceptive foe, you might miss obvious cues or be caught off guard, leading to an Impairment as your structured thinking fails to account for the unpredictable.==
